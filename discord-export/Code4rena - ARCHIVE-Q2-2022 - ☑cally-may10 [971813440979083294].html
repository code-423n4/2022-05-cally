C4 is currently expanding their group of judges to handle an increasing number of contests. I expect this timeline to be further streamlined in the future.
18:59
I think right now there is a sort of bottleneck whenever a contest changes hands. Currently it is set up as:
  • Sponsor reviews wardens findings and disputes/disagrees with issues and attaches relevant comments.
  • Judges go through and make the decision based on information provided by the sponsor. Sometimes there is a bit of back and forth to come to a final decision. This can add some time as its all done asynchronously.
  • Judge finishes up and C4 allows for the sponsor to do a final review and double check things before awards are announced.
